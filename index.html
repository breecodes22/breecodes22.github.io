About Me

From launching the first predictive analytics models for care at Epic Systems to now, I have been captivated by the ways to use cutting-edge technologies like AI/ML and automation to improve patient access to care. I am guided by the belief that every person should have affordable and accessible quality healthcare when needed. Here are the problems I'm working to solve in my career: Why is medical care in the U.S.A. some of the best in the world, but access to care is not? Why do over 80% of medical bills contain errors? Why has AI not been adopted widely in the healthcare industry? I love to connect with others working on these problems, or are interested in discussing how to improve these areas in the future. Please reach out!
